==============================================
Senior Citizen & PWD Discount Calculator (PH)
==============================================

A simple calculator for computing dining discounts 
for Senior Citizens and Persons with Disabilities 
(PWDs) in the Philippines.

----------------------------------------------
ABOUT
----------------------------------------------
In the Philippines, Senior Citizens and PWDs are 
entitled to a 20% discount on their share of the bill 
when dining out. This tool helps users calculate 
the correct discount based on the number of qualified 
individuals and total diners.

Useful for:
- Diners who want to verify the discount
- Restaurant staff for quick calculations
- Anyone splitting a bill with Seniors/PWDs

----------------------------------------------
HOW TO USE
----------------------------------------------
1. Open index.html in your browser.
2. Enter the total bill amount.
3. Enter:
   - Number of Senior Citizens
   - Number of PWDs
   - Total number of diners
   - Number of people sharing the bill
4. Click "Calculate Discount"
5. The app will display:
   - Total Discount
   - Final Amount to Pay After Discount

NOTE:
- The app assumes equal sharing of the bill.
- Each eligible person gets a 20% discount on 
  THEIR SHARE ONLY.

----------------------------------------------
EXAMPLE
----------------------------------------------
Total bill: ₱1,000
Total diners: 5
Seniors: 2

Each person’s share = ₱1,000 / 5 = ₱200
Discount per qualified person = ₱200 x 0.2 = ₱40
Total discount = ₱40 x 2 = ₱80
Final bill = ₱1,000 - ₱80 = ₱920
